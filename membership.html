Frequently Asked Questions

Application

Q. What are the eligibility requirements?

We seek applicants with a strong interest in financial engineering. A minimum commitment of one year is required, except for those preparing for graduate study abroad.

Q. Is the application process conducted in person?

No — all application procedures are conducted online via Zoom.

Q. Can I submit my application in Korean?

Yes, you may submit your application in Korean.

Q. Can I apply without programming experience?

Basic proficiency in Python is required, but prior experience is not. If you are new to Python, we recommend learning the basics before applying.

Q. Are graduate students eligible to apply?

Yes. Undergraduates, graduate students, and working professionals are all welcome to apply.

Q. How much mathematics knowledge is required?

A basic understanding of calculus and a course in probability theory are recommended, but not strictly required.

Q. Do I need to schedule my interview time?

After the document screening stage, we will contact you via email to coordinate the Zoom interview schedule.

Q. When will I know the result of my application?

Final results are sent via email on the last day of interviews.

Q. What is the membership fee?

A one-time lifetime fee of KRW 100,000, which covers ChatGPT Pro, Grammarly, and Mathpix subscriptions. A separate refundable activity deposit applies for active participation.

Sessions

Q. Are sessions conducted in English?

Sessions are held in Korean, but all educational materials are provided in English.

Q. How many sessions must I take?

In the first semester, one Core session and one Specialization session are mandatory. In the second semester, you may freely take one additional Specialization.

Q. Are sessions held in person?

No — all sessions are conducted online via Zoom, on weekend evenings.
